About us

Who We Are

AEROLOGISTICS & TRAVELS LTD is one of the fastest growing Nigerian Travel, Tour & Aviation Logistics Company. AEROLOGISTICS was incorporated in January 06 2006 and primarily engaging in the following businesses among others namely: The business of travel agents, tour operators, flight logistics, carriage of passengers and freight, Supply Chain / Logistics Management, procurers of over flyer and landing permit.

AEROLOGISTICS is an accredited IATA member and belong to the following distinguished bodies namely: Nigeria American Chamber of Commerce, Lagos Chamber of Commerce and Industries, Networking United States of America (NUSA), Association of Foreign Airlines Representatives in Nigeria (AFARN), National Association of Nigerian Travel Agents (NANTA), Nigerian Tourism Development Corporation (NTDC) among others.
